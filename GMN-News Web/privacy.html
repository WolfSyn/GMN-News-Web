Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 5, 2025

1. Introduction

GMN News (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the GMN News website and mobile app (the “Service”). We’re committed to protecting your privacy and explaining how we collect, use, and share information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, timestamps.
  • Device Data: Device model, operating system, browser type.
  • Analytics: We may use third-party analytics (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand usage patterns.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: To store preferences and improve user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver and maintain the Service (fetching and displaying gaming news).
  • To improve the Service (feature usage, performance monitoring).
  • To communicate updates, security alerts, and support messages.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with external APIs (e.g., GameSpot, YouTube, Yahoo RSS) to fetch news and videos. Each provider has its own privacy practices—please review their policies before use.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers under contract (hosting, analytics).
  • To comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.

6. Your Choices

  • You can disable cookies via your browser settings (may affect functionality).
  • Opt out of analytics tracking—see our Support page for details.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their personal data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy—changes will be posted here with a revised “Last updated” date.
